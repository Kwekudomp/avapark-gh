03 — Bookings

Managing Bookings

Every guest booking appears here. You can confirm, cancel, search, filter, and contact guests directly.

📌 How a Booking Works

👤

Guest Books

Fills form on the website and pays or reserves

→
⏳

Pending

Appears in your dashboard as Pending

→
👀

You Review

Check the date, group size, and notes

→
✅

Confirm

Click Confirm — guest gets WhatsApp message

→
🎉

Guest Arrives

They show up on the booking date

Understanding Booking Statuses

⏳ Pending New booking, not yet actioned by admin
✅ Confirmed You've confirmed — guest is expected to arrive
❌ Cancelled Booking was cancelled (by admin or guest)

Action Buttons Explained

Confirm Marks booking as confirmed. Only shown on Pending bookings.
Cancel Marks booking as cancelled. Guest is not automatically notified — use WA to inform them.
WA Opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message to the guest's phone number.

Searching & Filtering

🔍

Search bar

Type any part of a guest's name, email, phone number, or experience name.

🏷️

Status filter

Click All / Pending / Confirmed / Cancelled to show only that group.

📅

Date range

Set a From and To date to see bookings only within that period.

✖️

Clear filters

Click Clear filters to reset everything and see all bookings again.

⚠️

Pay at Venue bookings

If a guest chose "Reserve — Pay at Venue", the deposit column shows GHC 0. The notes field will say how much is due on arrival. Collect this when they arrive.

04 — Experiences

Managing Experiences

Control which experiences are visible on the site and which appear as featured on the homepage.

1

Go to Experiences

From the dashboard, click the Experiences card.

2

Toggle Featured

Click Featured on an experience to show/hide it in the homepage highlights grid (max 6 recommended).

3

Toggle Active

Click Active to show or hide an experience from the public site entirely. Hidden experiences won't appear anywhere.

4

Edit an Experience

Click Edit to update the name, description, price, images, schedule, or package details.

Featured vs Active

⭐ Featured = ON
Appears in the homepage "Discover What Awaits" grid
⭐ Featured = OFF
Only visible on the /experiences page, not homepage
👁️ Active = ON
Visible to the public on the site
🚫 Active = OFF
Hidden from the public. Use for seasonal or paused experiences.
💡

Tip

Keep 4–6 experiences featured on the homepage. Too many makes it look cluttered.

05 — Gallery

Uploading Photos

Add real photos of the park to the public gallery. Images are stored securely in Supabase and served via CDN.

1

Go to Gallery

From the dashboard, click the Gallery card.

2

Add a description

Type a short description in the Description field (e.g. "Pool at sunset").

3

Choose a category

Select from: venue · camping · pool · gardens · events

4

Click "Choose Photo"

Select the image file from your device. Upload starts automatically.

5

Delete if needed

Hover over any photo and click the ✕ button to remove it.

📋 File Requirements

Formats JPG, PNG, WEBP
Max file size 50 MB
Recommended size 1920×1280 or larger
Storage Supabase CDN (fast)
📸

Best practice

Upload bright, high-quality photos. Avoid blurry or dark images — they represent the park to potential visitors.

06 — Events

Creating Upcoming Events

Add special events that appear on the homepage and drive bookings. Events show automatically until the date has passed.

1

Click "+ Add Event"

Button in the top-right of the Events page.

2

Enter the event title

Keep it short and exciting. E.g. "New Year's Eve Bonfire Night"

3

Set the date(s)

Enter the Start Date. For multi-day events, set an End Date too.

4

Add price & description

Enter the price (e.g. GHC 200) and a short description of what to expect.

5

Upload a cover image

Click Upload Image to add a photo. This appears as the event card image on the site.

6

Click "Save Event"

The event goes live immediately on the homepage (if date is in the future).

🗓️ Event Fields

Title *
Name of the event shown to visitors
Start Date *
When the event begins
End Date
For multi-day events (optional)
Price
e.g. GHC 150. Leave blank for free events.
Ticket Link
URL to buy tickets (optional). Shows "Get Tickets" button.
Cover Image
Photo shown on the event card
📌

Auto-hide past events

Events with a past start date are automatically hidden from the homepage. They remain in the admin list so you have a record.

09 — Guest Reviews

⭐ Managing Reviews

Guests can submit reviews from the homepage. Every review starts as Pending — only you decide what gets published on the site.

⏳

Pending

Newly submitted reviews. Not visible to the public yet. These trigger the red badge on your dashboard.

✅

Approved

Approved reviews appear live on the homepage "What Our Guests Say" section immediately.

🚫

Rejected

Rejected reviews are hidden from the public and kept in your records. Use this for spam or inappropriate content.

Review Lifecycle

📝

on the homepage
→
⏳

🔴 badge appears
→
👀
You review it
at /admin/reviews
→
✅
Approve → Live
🚫
Reject → Hidden

How to Approve or Reject a Review

  1. 1
    Go to the Dashboard — if there are pending reviews, a red badge shows on the ⭐ Reviews card.
  2. 2
    Click the Reviews card to go to /admin/reviews.
  3. 3
    The Pending tab is selected by default. Read each review — the guest's name, which experience they visited, star rating, and comment are all shown.
  4. 4
    Click Approve to publish the review on the homepage, or Reject to hide it.
    ✓ Approve ✕ Reject
  5. 5
    The status updates instantly. Approved reviews go live on the homepage immediately — no page refresh needed.

💡 Tips

  • •Check reviews daily — a quick response time shows guests their feedback is valued.
  • •Use the Pending filter tab to see only reviews needing action.
  • •Reject reviews that contain spam, offensive language, or are clearly fake.
  • •You can change a rejected review to approved later if needed — just click Approve.
  • •Aim to have at least 5–10 approved reviews visible — social proof builds booking confidence.
